FIFA

What is FIFA?

FIFA is a video game in which players can play soccer games against either the computer or humans. It simulates what a real soccer game would be like and each player or computer can control the players on the field to try to beat their opponent.

Why is it one of my favorites?

FIFA has come to be one of my favorite games for a few reasons:

  • First, as a kid I played a lot of soccer both for fun and competitively. This game me an initial interest in FIFA the video game.
  • Second, my interest in soccer made me interested in professional soccer and within the game players are on their actual teams and have attributes based on their real life performance and abilities.
  • Finally, FIFA's gameplay is very simple to start and unbelieveably complex to master. This attracted all of my non soccer playing friends to begin playing FIFA. Playing with friends is not only a reason I love the game but a large reason why most fans like the FIFA series.
